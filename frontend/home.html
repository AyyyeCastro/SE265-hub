THE PROCESS


As the sole creator of PlugIn, all responsibilities fell upon me. I started by brainstorming project ideas. After many drafts were left behind, one project stood out- PlugIn. I began by prototyping the web designs in Figma, and getting an understanding of the database designs through diagramining.

After the initial drafts were created, I brought my project fourth for review by peers and professionals. Several implementations were changed to better the design, into what PlugIn is today.
